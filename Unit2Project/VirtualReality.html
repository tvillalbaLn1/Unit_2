Virtual reality

Virtual reality (VR) is a simulated experience that employs 3D near-eye displays and pose tracking to give the user an immersive feel of a virtual world.

VR is one of the key technologies in the reality-virtuality continuum. As such, it is different from other digital visualization solutions, such as augmented virtuality and augmented reality.

I find it interesting how the general public thinks that VR, just like AI, is something that was developed just recently when, in reality, the first VRs set date back to the 1970s.
