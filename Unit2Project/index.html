Technology is defined as "the practical application of knowledge especially in a particular area: engineering."

As knowledge is always evolving, hence so is technology. Technology is traditionally related to the future. However, it is important to note that technology itself has always existed since the birth of humankind, it exist in the present moment and it will continue to exist into the future.

In this website you'll find information about three subjects where technology is constantly evolving: robotics, virtual reality and the internet of things.

The latest buzz around technology: Artificial Intelligence(AI)
